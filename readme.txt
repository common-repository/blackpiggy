BlackPiggy is a wordpress plugin that allow you  install into your blog the blackpiggy widget.
BlackPiggy offers to small businesses:
- the ability to put deals on their websites.
- the ability to promote deals with other releated deals of different vendors.
- a gamification platform that converts customers into active promoters

ttp://www.blackpiggy.com

== Installation ==

1. Upload to your plugins folder, usually `wp-content/plugins/`
2. Activate the plugin on the plugin screen
3. Click on Setting and insert your iddeal (to have an active deal please contact info.company@blackpiggy.com)
